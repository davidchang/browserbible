Ecclesiastes 1

103

1 Praise Yahweh, my soul! 2 Praise Yahweh, my soul, 3 who forgives all your sins; 4 who redeems your life from destruction; 5 who satisfies your desire with good things, 6 Yahweh executes righteous acts, 7 He made known his ways to Moses, 8 Yahweh is merciful and gracious, 9 He will not always accuse; 10 He has not dealt with us according to our sins, 11 For as the heavens are high above the earth, 12 As far as the east is from the west, 13 Like a father has compassion on his children, 14 For he knows how we are made. 15 As for man, his days are like grass. 16 For the wind passes over it, and it is gone. 17 But Yahweh’s loving kindness is from everlasting to everlasting with those who fear him, 18 to those who keep his covenant, 19 Yahweh has established his throne in the heavens. 20 Praise Yahweh, you angels of his, 21 Praise Yahweh, all you armies of his, 22 Praise Yahweh, all you works of his,
