Ecclesiastes 1

75

1 We give thanks to you, God. 2 When I choose the appointed time, 3 The earth and all its inhabitants quake. 4 I said to the arrogant, “Don’t boast!” 5 Don’t lift up your horn on high. 6 For neither from the east, nor from the west, 7 But God is the judge. 8 For in the hand of Yahweh there is a cup, 9 But I will declare this forever: 10 I will cut off all the horns of the wicked,
