Ecclesiastes 1

66

1 Make a joyful shout to God, all the earth! 2 Sing to the glory of his name! 3 Tell God, “How awesome are your deeds! 4 All the earth will worship you, 5 Come, and see God’s deeds— 6 He turned the sea into dry land. 7 He rules by his might forever. 8 Praise our God, you peoples! 9 who preserves our life among the living, 10 For you, God, have tested us. 11 You brought us into prison. 12 You allowed men to ride over our heads. 13 I will come into your temple with burnt offerings. 14 which my lips promised, 15 I will offer to you burnt offerings of fat animals, 16 Come, and hear, all you who fear God. 17 I cried to him with my mouth. 18 If I cherished sin in my heart, 19 But most certainly, God has listened. 20 Blessed be God, who has not turned away my prayer,
