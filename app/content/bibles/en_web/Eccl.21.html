Ecclesiastes 21 (WEB)

Song of Songs 1

21

1 The king’s heart is in Yahweh’s hand like the watercourses. 2 Every way of a man is right in his own eyes, 3 To do righteousness and justice 4 A high look, and a proud heart, 5 The plans of the diligent surely lead to profit; 6 Getting treasures by a lying tongue 7 The violence of the wicked will drive them away, 8 The way of the guilty is devious, 9 It is better to dwell in the corner of the housetop, 10 The soul of the wicked desires evil; 11 When the mocker is punished, the simple gains wisdom. 12 The Righteous One considers the house of the wicked, 13 Whoever stops his ears at the cry of the poor, 14 A gift in secret pacifies anger; 15 It is joy to the righteous to do justice; 16 The man who wanders out of the way of understanding 17 He who loves pleasure shall be a poor man. 18 The wicked is a ransom for the righteous; 19 It is better to dwell in a desert land, 20 There is precious treasure and oil in the dwelling of the wise; 21 He who follows after righteousness and kindness 22 A wise man scales the city of the mighty, 23 Whoever guards his mouth and his tongue 24 The proud and haughty man, “scoffer” is his name; 25 The desire of the sluggard kills him, 26 There are those who covet greedily all day long; 27 The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination: 28 A false witness will perish, 29 A wicked man hardens his face; 30 There is no wisdom nor understanding 31 The horse is prepared for the day of battle;
