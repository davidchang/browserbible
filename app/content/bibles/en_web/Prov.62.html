Ecclesiastes 1

62

1 My soul rests in God alone. 2 He alone is my rock and my salvation, my fortress— 3 How long will you assault a man, 4 They fully intend to throw him down from his lofty place. 5 My soul, wait in silence for God alone, 6 He alone is my rock and my salvation, my fortress. 7 With God is my salvation and my honor. 8 Trust in him at all times, you people. 9 Surely men of low degree are just a breath, 10 Don’t trust in oppression. 11 God has spoken once; 12 Also to you, Lord, belongs loving kindness,
