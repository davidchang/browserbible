Ecclesiastes 1

146

1 Praise Yah! 2 While I live, I will praise Yahweh. 3 Don’t put your trust in princes, 4 His spirit departs, and he returns to the earth. 5 Happy is he who has the God of Jacob for his help, 6 who made heaven and earth, 7 who executes justice for the oppressed; 8 Yahweh opens the eyes of the blind. 9 Yahweh preserves the foreigners. 10 Yahweh will reign forever;
