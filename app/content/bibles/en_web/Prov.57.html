Ecclesiastes 1

57

1 Be merciful to me, God, be merciful to me, 2 I cry out to God Most High, 3 He will send from heaven, and save me, 4 My soul is among lions. 5 Be exalted, God, above the heavens! 6 They have prepared a net for my steps. 7 My heart is steadfast, God, my heart is steadfast. 8 Wake up, my glory! Wake up, psaltery and harp! 9 I will give thanks to you, Lord, among the peoples. 10 For your great loving kindness reaches to the heavens, 11 Be exalted, God, above the heavens.
