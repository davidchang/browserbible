6

1 Listen now to what Yahweh says: 2 Hear, you mountains, Yahweh’s controversy, 3 My people, what have I done to you? 4 For I brought you up out of the land of Egypt, 5 My people, remember now what Balak king of Moab devised, 6 How shall I come before Yahweh, 7 Will Yahweh be pleased with thousands of rams? 8 He has shown you, O man, what is good. 9 Yahweh’s voice calls to the city, 10 Are there yet treasures of wickedness in the house of the wicked, 11 Shall I be pure with dishonest scales, 12 Her rich men are full of violence, 13 Therefore I also have struck you with a grievous wound. 14 You shall eat, but not be satisfied. 15 You will sow, but won’t reap. 16 For the statutes of Omri are kept,
