Ecclesiastes 1

135

1 Praise Yah! 2 you who stand in the house of Yahweh, 3 Praise Yah, for Yahweh is good. 4 For Yah has chosen Jacob for himself; 5 For I know that Yahweh is great, 6 Whatever Yahweh pleased, that he has done, 7 who causes the clouds to rise from the ends of the earth; 8 Who struck the firstborn of Egypt, 9 Who sent signs and wonders into your midst, Egypt, 10 who struck many nations, 11 Sihon king of the Amorites, 12 and gave their land for a heritage, 13 Your name, Yahweh, endures forever; 14 For Yahweh will judge his people, 15 The idols of the nations are silver and gold, 16 They have mouths, but they can’t speak. 17 They have ears, but they can’t hear; 18 Those who make them will be like them; 19 House of Israel, praise Yahweh! 20 House of Levi, praise Yahweh! 21 Blessed be Yahweh from Zion,
